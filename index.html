About Us

Madina Cloth House

Madina Cloth House is a family-run gents and ladies clothing shop located in the heart of Sadar Bazar, Narang Mandi.

We deal in quality fabrics and ready-to-wear for men and women, with a special focus on proper fitting, premium textures, and affordable prices.

Beyond our daily collections, we take pride in our exclusive Bridal Variety. From intricately embroidered fancy suits to heavy festive wear, we offer elegant designs that make your special occasions truly memorable.
